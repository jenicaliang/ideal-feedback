Thank you for taking a moment to share your response.

The material you just reviewed presents IDEAL, a speculative concept developed as part of a Communication Design Capstone project. This form is intended to capture your impressions, questions, and concerns about the idea of the service.

All responses are anonymous.

Please note:

IDEAL is a hypothetical project, not a real product or company. The devices and services described are not currently available, and the system is not technically feasible in its proposed form today.

However, the concept is informed by real developments—such as increasingly capable decision-making AI systems and the growing accuracy of personal data tracking technologies (e.g., wearables like Oura).

This project is less interested in whether IDEAL could exist, and more interested in how people respond to the idea of such a service.
